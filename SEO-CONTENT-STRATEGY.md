# SEO Content Strategy for Habtam Limited

## Phase 1: Content Creation (Weeks 1-4)

### Blog Posts (High-Value Keywords)

#### 1. "Kenya's Tea Grades Explained: FOP, GFOP, CTC, Dust & More"
- Target Keywords: Kenya tea grades, FOP tea, GFOP tea, CTC tea grades
- Word Count: 1,500+ words
- Content:
  - Introduction to Kenyan tea grading system
  - Detailed explanation of each grade (FOP, GFOP, OPA, CTC, Fannings, Dust)
  - Quality indicators and pricing differences
  - Visual guide comparing grades
  - FAQ section
  - Internal links to product pages

#### 2. "Purple Tea: Kenya's Superfood - Health Benefits & Brewing Guide"
- Target Keywords: Purple tea Kenya, anthocyanin tea, health benefits tea
- Word Count: 1,200+ words
- Content:
  - What is purple tea and its origins
  - Scientific health benefits (anthocyanins, antioxidants)
  - Brewing methods and temperature
  - Comparison with black and green tea
  - Product details and ordering info

#### 3. "How to Choose Premium Kenyan Tea: Complete Buyer's Guide"
- Target Keywords: Buy Kenyan tea, premium tea export, tea buyer's guide
- Word Count: 1,400+ words
- Content:
  - Tea sourcing from Kenya's highlands
  - Quality indicators to look for
  - Storage and freshness tips
  - Wholesale vs retail pricing
  - Habtam Limited expertise advantages
  - Case studies or customer testimonials

#### 4. "From Kenya's Highlands to Your Cup: The Tea Processing Journey"
- Target Keywords: Kenyan tea processing, tea production, tea manufacturing
- Word Count: 1,300+ words
- Content:
  - Tea plant cultivation in Kenya
  - Harvest season and hand-picking process
  - Withering and fermentation
  - Modern processing technology
  - Quality control procedures
  - Sustainability practices

### Product Pages (To Create/Enhance)

#### Per Tea Grade:
- FOP (Flowery Orange Pekoe) Grade
- GFOP (Golden Flowery Orange Pekoe) Grade
- OPA (Orange Pekoe A) Grade
- CTC Black Tea (Dust, Fannings, BP1, PF1)
- White Silver Tips
- Purple Tea
- Oolong Tea

Each page should include:
- SEO Title: "[Grade Name] Kenyan Tea | Premium Grade | Habtam Limited"
- Meta Description: 150-160 characters, including grade name and key benefits
- Grade specifications (oxidation %, leaf size, color)
- Flavor profile and brewing guide
- Health benefits
- Bulk pricing information
- Customer reviews/testimonials
- Call-to-action (Order Now / Request Quote)
- Internal links to related grades and blog posts
- Schema markup (Product schema)
